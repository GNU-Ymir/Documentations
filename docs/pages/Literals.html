2.1 Literals and operators

Integers 1, Floats 1.2, Characters 'a', Strings "abc", Bools true can be expressed as literals.

Integer literal is sequence of digit optionally followed by a suffix. The suffix specify the size of the integer data, by default without the suffix, it is an int of size 32-bits.
  • B and UB for respectively byte and ubyte (8-bits).
  • S and US for respectively short and ushort (16-bits).
  • U for uint (32-bits).
  • L and UL for respectively long and ulong (64-bits).
